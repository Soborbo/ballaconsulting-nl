Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about cross-border investments and company formation.

How long does it take to register a company in Hungary?
Typically 2-3 weeks from document preparation to registration completion. With proper preparation and all documents in order, the actual registration can be completed within 5-7 business days. I handle all the documentation, translations, and notary requirements to ensure a smooth process.
What is the minimum investment required for Hungarian property?
There is no legal minimum investment amount, but I typically recommend starting with properties in the €50,000-€150,000 range for first-time investors. This range offers good entry points in Budapest and secondary cities with strong rental yields and appreciation potential.
Do I need to speak Hungarian to invest in Hungarian real estate?
No, language is not a barrier. I provide full translation services and manage all communications with local authorities, lawyers, and contractors. All documentation can be prepared in English or Dutch, and I ensure you understand every step of the process.
What are the tax benefits of establishing a Hungarian company?
Hungary offers the lowest corporate tax rate in the EU at 9%, compared to 25.8% in the Netherlands. Additionally, there are favorable holding company structures, R&D tax credits, and specific incentives for certain industries. I help structure your business to maximize these benefits legally and efficiently.
Can you help with property renovation and management?
Yes, I provide end-to-end renovation management services. This includes contractor selection, project oversight, quality control, and budget management. I also offer property management services for rental properties, including tenant selection, rent collection, and maintenance coordination.
What are the ongoing costs of maintaining a Hungarian company?
Annual costs typically include accounting services (€1,200-€2,500), registered office address (€500-€1,000), and tax filing requirements. These costs are significantly lower than Netherlands equivalents, and I can connect you with trusted local service providers at competitive rates.
How do you charge for your services?
My fee structure is transparent and project-based. For company registration, I charge a fixed fee. For property investments, I work on a success-based commission model aligned with your investment goals. Initial consultations are complimentary to ensure we're a good fit before any commitment.
What makes your approach different from other consultants?
I'm a Dutch resident with deep Hungarian roots and 15+ years of hands-on experience in both markets. Unlike large consulting firms, I provide personalized, boutique-level service and treat every investment as if it were my own. My established local network gives you access to off-market opportunities and insider market knowledge.
